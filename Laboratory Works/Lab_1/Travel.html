Travel to Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact is an open-world action role -playing game developed and published by miHoYo.
The game features a fantasy open-world environment and action-based battle system using elemental magic and
character-switching, and uses gacha game monetization for players to obtain new characters, weapons, and other resources.

Gameplay

Genshin Impact is a game that allows the player to control one of four interchangeable characters in a party. Switching between characters can be done quickly during combat, allowing the player to use several different combinations of skills and attacks.
