Recipe #1

  • Time: 60 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Bread 1
Tomato 2

Fisherman's Toast is a food item that the player can cook. The recipe for Fisherman's Toast is available from the start of the game.Depending on the quality, Fisherman's Toast increases the party's defense by 88/107/126 for 300 seconds. Like most foods, this has no effect for other players in Co-Op Mode; this effect only applies to the player's party..
